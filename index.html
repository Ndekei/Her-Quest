The Reality

She never stops
searching.

A woman is known to always be on a search; a quest for the best recipe, dress, home, shopping deal, beauty therapy, and not to forget, the perfect man! Sadly, it seems like she never gets to the treasure.

While on this quest, the woman oscillates between four forms of identity, each with different characteristics. It takes her through a roller coaster of spiritual and emotional experiences that leave her feeling lost and unsure of her identity.

4 Forms of Identity
Every Woman Navigates

The Answer

Anchored in the Word.
Designed for Discovery.

Purely anchored on the word of God, Her Quest is packaged as a series that explains these different forms of identity the woman takes and starts to provide answers to her lingering questions.

Join me as we journey and discover a woman's true identity as intended by God since creation — and how she can take her place and be effective, relevant and influential in God's agenda here on earth.

Meet the Founder

Naomi Makumi.

Naomi Makumi is a woman of deep faith, vision, and purpose. As the founder of Her Quest, she carries a God-given mandate to help women recover their true identity — not the one shaped by circumstance, but the one written by God before time began.

Through years of ministry, personal journey, and anchored study of the Word, Naomi has crafted a framework that meets women exactly where they are — and walks them, step by step, into who they were always meant to be.
